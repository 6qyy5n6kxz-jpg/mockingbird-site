The Mockingbird Wine Café

Mockingbird is a wine café in Graytown focused on from-scratch food and a bottle-forward list. We keep the atmosphere cozy and welcoming so neighbors and friends can drop in for dinner or linger over a glass.

Our menu is cooked in-house: homemade soups, flatbreads, pressed sandwiches, grilled cheese, and weekly specials. Pair them with curated wines from around the world, craft beer, ciders, sangria, meads, and thoughtful non-alcoholic options.

We keep things simple: walk in or call ahead. Want to plan ahead? Check our events for ticketed dinners and tastings.

  • Nearly 100 bottles spanning classic regions and new finds
  • Rotating glass pours, sangria, beer, and NA picks
  • From-scratch kitchen with seasonal specials each week
  • Warm, local hospitality — no pretense, no fuss
